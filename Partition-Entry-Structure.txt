# Partition Entry = 16 Bytes

Offset | Size (Byte) | Description
0      | 1           | Bootable (0x80) / Non-bootable (0x00)
1      | 3           | Starting CHS
4      | 1           | Partition Type
5      | 3           | Ending CHS
8      | 4           | Relative Sector (LBA Start)
12     | 4           | Number of Sectors in Partition
